Episode 1: Introduction
Ali, Ishita, Maurice and Nav introduce themselves and discuss how school affects their mental health.
Episode 2: School and Faith
Ali, Ishita and Nav discuss how they feel after exams, and Maurice discusses moving to VCE. They also discuss faith in relation to mental health.
Episode 3: Social Media
Ali, Ishita and Nav discuss the harms social media can cause, and how to manage a social media addiction.
Episode 4: Learning Styles
Ali, Ishita, Nav and Maurice discuss the different types of learning styles and the issues with school and how it fails to cater to specific learning styles.
